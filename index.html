My First Website

Creativity has always been a key part of who I am. Whether I’m building, repairing, or picking up entirely new skills, I thrive on turning ideas into tangible results.

Recently, I’ve been teaching myself to code. It’s intimidating but exciting, and this site serves as both a learning tool and a way to share the many hobbies and skills I’ve picked up along the way. I’m hopeful that this experience will lead to something meaningful..
